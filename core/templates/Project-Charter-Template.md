**Relationship to Other Documents**

- **Vision & Positioning** = external "why" (marketing, stakeholders)
- **Project Charter** = internal design document (shared understanding between user and agents)
- **Phase Definitions** = scope breakdown across phases, referenced by PRDs

All three must be tightly aligned. The Charter captures what the
product IS; V&P captures why it matters to the outside world; Phase
Definitions break the Charter's capabilities into buildable phases.
